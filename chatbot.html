The Three Doshas

Your unique
constitution

In Ayurveda, every individual is a unique combination of three biological energies — Vata, Pitta, and Kapha — known as doshas. Understanding yours is the foundation of all healing.

V
Vata
Air + Space
Governs movement, creativity, and the nervous system. Vata types are quick, imaginative, and sensitive.
P
Pitta
Fire + Water
Governs digestion, intelligence, and transformation. Pitta types are sharp, driven, and intense.
K
Kapha
Earth + Water
Governs structure, immunity, and stability. Kapha types are calm, nurturing, and grounded.
